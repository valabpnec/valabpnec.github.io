Introduction

Virtual Reality Center (VRC) was established in 2020 and has the ability to provide different immersive technology solutions that will be much lower in cost and have different levels of immersion. Some of these services include virtual orientation, training simulator, design review, session-based training and maintenance support, etc. The timeline of the development of VR solutions varies based on the type of services required. Though, immersive technology-based software solutions' timeline is considerably less than its hardware counterpart.

VRC is being headed by Dr. Rana Hammad Raza; who is currently serving as Assistant Professor at PNEC NUST.

On-demand Services

We provide a wide array of services for commercialization purpose

Virtual Orientation of Campus

This service will provide virtual tours or walkarounds of Campus/Vessels using VR simulation technology in a 360-degree panoramic display

Simulation-based Training for Operators

This service includes operators training on specialized equipment in VR according to the underlying requirements

Design Review

This service provides an essential aid to avoid any unforeseen design flaws (such as structural deficiencies of a building/ equipment) later in the construction stage

Session-based Training

VR session-based training can be used for Damage Control and Firefighting training etc

Maintenance Support

This service can be used to train personnel in the virtual environment to familiarize them with what tools to use for specific scenarios

Miscellaneous

VR technology can also be used for training personnel for various medical situations such as CPR etc. Moreover, VR-based psychological therapy is also being effectively utilized.

We aspire to develop intelligent algorithms that perform important visual perception tasks.
