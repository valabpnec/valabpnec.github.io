Importance of Deepfakes Creation, Detection and Disruption

  • 10th July 2014
  • admin
  • General, Media
  • 43 Comments

The term “deepfake” is derived from the term deep learning, Deepfakes are synthetic media in which a person in an image or video is replaced with someone else’s resemblance. In short, deepfakes make it possible to swap one person’s face in an image or video with face of another person.

There are several methods for creating deepfakes, however all of them employ artificial intelligence but the most common is by using a deep neural network involving autoencoders.
